Import Events from Spreadsheet

Paste rows from your STC 2026 All-Events Tracker spreadsheet below. Copy the data rows (not the header), tab-separated. The importer expects columns in this order:

Complete (TRUE/FALSE) | Date | Place | Activity | Park Section | Time | # Volunteers | Company Name | Social Media | Point of Contact | Email | Check-in Email Sent | Actual # Volunteers | One-Pager Sent | Parks Needs | Staff 1 | Staff 2 | Staff 3 | Paid

Imported events will be added to your existing events. Duplicates (same date + company) will be skipped.
